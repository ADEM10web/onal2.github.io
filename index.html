mythical Zoan
Mythical Zoan (「動物ゾオン系」幻獣種, Zōn-kei Genjūshu?, literally meaning "Animal System Mythical Beast Species") is a sub-class of Zoan abilities that allow users to transform into creatures that are the subject of myth and legend. Mythical Zoans are regarded as rarer than Logia-type Devil Fruits and, in accordance with this rarity, are considered by some to be the strongest Devil Fruit type. Mythical Zoans demonstrate some of the most varied properties among Devil Fruits: on top of the transformation itself, most come with "mythical" abilities that are Paramecia-like in nature. Examples include the daibutsu Zoan's ability to project shockwaves, the phoenix Zoan's ability to heal using blue flames, and the nine-tailed fox Zoan's ability to transform into someone's lookalike (heavily resembling a certain Paramecia power). Some also allow the user to control elements, though these work differently to Logia-created ones—i.e., the user can only project the element, not transform into it, thus lacking the amorphous bodies of Logia users. Some Mythical Zoan users have demonstrated access to their special abilities without invoking their transformation; the Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika, in particular, permanently transforms the consumer's body into a rubbery state, akin to the lasting effects of certain body-altering Paramecias. Finally, a Mythical Zoan fruit may bestow more than just one special ability, e.g.: Kaidou is able to create and control different elements in his Azure Dragon form (fire, lightning, and wind), as well as conjure Flame Clouds to make himself and other things fly.
